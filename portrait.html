Portrait

Photography

Portrait photography is a genre focused on capturing the essence and personality of individuals or groups. It emphasizes the subject's expressions, poses, and emotions to create compelling and evocative images. Whether in a studio setting or amidst a natural backdrop, portrait photography seeks to tell a unique story through the faces and characteristics of its subjects.
