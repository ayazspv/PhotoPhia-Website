Nature

Photography

Nature photography is the art of capturing the beauty and diversity of the natural world through the lens of a camera. It encompasses a wide range of subjects, from landscapes and wildlife to plants and weather phenomena. Nature photographers aim to evoke a sense of wonder and appreciation for the environment, often highlighting the intricate details and grandeur of the Earth's ecosystems.
